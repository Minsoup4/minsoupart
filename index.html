About The Artist

Minsoup, also known as Josh, is a 16 year old highschool student based in New York City. During his free time, he often works on drawings and paintings while listening to music, ranging anywhere from jazz instrumental, psych-pop, ambient music, electronic, disco, and more. Josh's works range from a variety of subjects using mediums including charcoal on paper, paint on canvas, graphite on paper, and ink on paper.

Artist's Statement

Drawing is one of the many forms of storytelling. It has the potential to be used in many ways, and it can often say what words cannot. I draw with this in mind- I draw with the purpose of observing life and interpreting it in a way which may tell a meaningful story to an audience. I hope you may find a meaningful story which relates to you inside my artwork, and I wish you a good day. Thank you very much for your interest in my works. There are many more to come surely :)
